MONSTERS' ABYSS EVOLUTION

2015

It all started when Aurora came to live to London with a bunch of uncertain songs and casually met Irene. During rainy days - mostly every day - they played songs together to pass the time.

2016

They decided that it was high time to see the light and started to play in some open-mics. But the name of the band was still missing so they asked for help from their audience to vote for a proper one. This is how Monsters Abyss was born.

2017

And Jake got into the scene. A friend of a friend introduced Jake to the band. He started playing the bass-guitar but his great guitar skills suddenly surprised the two members and he was forced to change the instrument.

2018

And then, came the most expected moment. The band found a drummer and played their first gig. It was a very exciting and scary moment but for sure unforgetful

2019

And the experience was addictive! Monsters' Abyss started performing and having fun everywhere they could go! They made a nice tour around London center pubs.

2021

After a little break, Monsters' Abyss is coming with exciting news, more changes and new members.
