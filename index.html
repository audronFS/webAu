About...

Aurora Del Rio is a dynamic musician always looking for new challenges and innovative ways of creating music. Despite have studied a music degree specialise on classical violin and played in many orchestras and ensembles, she has always felt very appealed by the composition and started making pop and rock songs at an early age.

When she arrived to London she realised that it was a great city to break rules and created Monstersâ€™ Abyss, an indie pop rock band where the clarity of a classical violin is combined with the distorted sound of the electric guitars. She also had the opportunity to met exceptional musician with whom grow and deeply connect.

Recently, and after taking a MA on music composition with New Technologies, she found the astonishing world of the electronic music, a world where sound and texture overcome other music elements. This has meant a new way of composing music for here. Thanks to new technologies, Aurora has found a perfect tool to combine features of the Classical and pop music all together with an innovative perspective and a search of an own style and music personality.
